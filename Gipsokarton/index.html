How It Calculates & Practical Tips

📄 Drywall Layers & Waste Factor

A partition wall is always sheeted on both sides. Choosing 2 layers per side automatically calculates 4 total drywall layers over the partition footprint. Waste ratio is defaulted at 10%.

⚙️ Profile Specifications (CW/UW & CD/UD)

Wall claddings and partitions utilize vertical CW studs and perimeter UW channels. Suspended ceilings use main CD channels and perimeter UD tracks.

🔒 Why use PRO Mode?

PRO mode unlocks accurate fastener counts (25mm screws for inner layer, 35mm for outer layer), wall plugs, hangers, joint tape, joint filler, along with labor cost calculations.
