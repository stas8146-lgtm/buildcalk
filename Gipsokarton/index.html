How It Calculates & Practical Tips

📄 Sheets & Structural Integrity

Our calculator automatically appends a standard 10% material buffer to accommodate cuttings, cuts, and irregular angles. A standard drywall sheet dimensions are 1.2 x 2.5 meters (3 m² area). If you utilize double-layering for fireproofing or advanced acoustic insulation, the sheet estimate will automatically double.

⚙️ Profiles (CD & UD Skeleton Frame)

UD profile functions as the perimeter guiding track, computed directly from wall borders. CD profile works as the primary load-bearing vertically spaced guide. Our standard vertical pitch is 60cm, but we strongly recommend upgrading to 40cm spacing if your wall partition is designated to host tiling, heavy shelves, or multiple storage components.

🔒 Why use PRO Mode?

PRO mode utilizes comprehensive estimation mathematics to provide total count for hardware. It details the exact metal screws needed (plasterboard-to-metal screws spaced every 25cm), direct mounting hangers to minimize core wobelling, wall dowels for masonry connections, joint glassfiber tape, and joint filling compound to seal panels.
