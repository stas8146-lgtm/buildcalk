Calculation & Waste Guide

📐 Waste Formula

Waste percentage is dynamic: standard layout with small tiles requires 5%. Large formats (120x60) increase waste due to large offcuts. Diagonal and Herringbone patterns automatically add 5-10% to the buffer.

💧 Adhesive & Grout

Adhesive is calculated at 1.3 kg per m² per 1mm of solid layer. A 10mm trowel leaves approx 4-5mm solid glue. Grout uses a formula based on total joint length, width, and tile thickness.
