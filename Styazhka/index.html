About this Semi-Dry Floor Screed Calculator

This professional online estimator is designed for builders, contractors, and floor screed specialists in Europe. It provides highly accurate material calculations for cement and sand floor screed projects.

Technical Specifications & Formula Parameters:

  • Cement Consumption: Calculated based on standard European Cement Class 32.5 (CEM I/II).
  • Sand Requirements: Optimized for standard screed 0-4 mm fraction sand (with small pebbles).
  • Additives & Fiber: Includes calculations for fiber reinforcement and plasticizer.
  • Financial Estimator: Real-time estimation of material costs, labor rates (work costs), and projected net profit.

Simply enter your area size (sqm) and floor screed thickness (mm) to get an instant, ready-to-use material and budget breakdown.
