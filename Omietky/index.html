How to use the Plaster Calculator

  1. Choose the calculation mode (Simple/Pro) and select the plaster type (Cement-Lime or Gypsum).
  2. Enter the total wall area in square meters (m²) and the estimated average thickness in millimeters (mm).
  3. In Pro Mode, adjust material prices, add external corners, window slopes (APU), and reinforcing mesh if needed.
  4. Personalize your PDF reports by entering your Company Name, Phone, and Email in the Print Settings.
  5. Click "Calculate" to see the results. Use "Save" to keep history, "PDF" to print a branded estimate, or "New" to reset.
